PRINCIPLES
TRUST THE PROCESS
real progress takes patience and consistency.
no shortcuts. the reps matter.
FLOW
match the difficulty to where the dog is right now.
too easy and they check out. too hard and they shut down.
BACKWARD CHAINING
break complex behaviors into small pieces.
train the last step first. the dog always finishes strong.
GENERALIZATION
a behavior learned in your living room means nothing at the park.
train across environments. that's when it becomes real.

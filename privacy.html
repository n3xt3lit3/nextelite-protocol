Overview

NextElite is a personal project by Nemine Steffensen. This privacy policy describes what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Data We Collect

Email addresses. If you subscribe to our mailing list, we collect your email address. Legal basis: your consent (GDPR Article 6(1)(a)). You can unsubscribe at any time by contacting nemine@nextelite.no.

Server logs. This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect basic server logs including IP addresses as part of their hosting infrastructure. See GitHub's privacy statement.

Local storage. This site may store preferences in your browser's localStorage. This does not involve third-party data transfer.

Cookies and Tracking

This site does not use cookies, analytics, or tracking pixels. No advertising or marketing cookies are deployed. No external font services or CDNs are used.

Your Rights Under GDPR

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

  • Right of access (Article 15) — Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Right to rectification (Article 16) — Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure (Article 17) — Request deletion of your data.
  • Right to restriction (Article 18) — Request limits on how we use your data.
  • Right to data portability (Article 20) — Request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to object (Article 21) — Object to processing of your data.
  • Right to withdraw consent — Where processing is based on consent, withdraw at any time.
